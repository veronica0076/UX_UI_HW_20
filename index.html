User Research

I favor user interviews in the research portion. I like to break the main objective into mini objectives and use that as a guide to make the interview more of a conversation. I find that it makes it easier for the interviewee to provide a full answer, making a significant difference in not only the interview but how much more it allows me to contribute to the project.

Prototyping

When prototyping, I like to test my problem solving skills. Nothing ever works correctly the first time, and I have adjusted my perspective to being an ideal situation. It allows me to not only explore and learn more about the tool im using but also gain insight with technique.

Presenting

This one is the most intimidating yet the most rewarding. I try my best to be engaging and make my projects into a story.
